How It Was Created

Design Process & Tools

SmartStudy was built using no-code tools focused on accessibility, creativity, and user experience.

Research & Planning

Identified student challenges: lack of motivation, poor time management, and exam stress. Focused on creating supportive solutions.

No-Code Tools

Used Canva for design, Google Forms for quizzes & feedback, and Zoho App Creator for structure.

Focus on Creativity

Focused on creativity, planning, and impact rather than complex programming to reach students effectively.

Positive Encouragement

Daily study tips, daily motivation, and positive messages support students emotionally and academically.

User Testing & Feedback

Collected real user feedback to measure impact and ensure the app meets its goal of lifting students up.

Adaptability & Growth

Developed problem-solving skills and learned to choose tools that match ability for positive impact.
